Sleeping Beauty

A tech-forward and family-friendly Mixed-Reality experience that lets visitors explore and interpret Dance Museum's Sleeping Beauty collections on-site and remotely. 

Video demo

Work presented by me, Shadan Sharifi, Katsiaryna Barysevich, and Ina Wong in 7 weeks | Mar. - Apr. 2024
I was responsible of Mixed-Reality development, Shader & VFX, voice-over and sound design.
Development used Unity, Meta Quest 3, and Git.
