Downdated City

Exploring adorable old times of your city by your phone on the go, a.k.a. life is not elsewhere. 

If your city has a long history,
and you'd explore just on the go ...


We need redepictions for ancient remains in their old-day prosperous shapes,
need hints of what happened on seemly normal streets whose ages are way longer than ours,
and just would like have deeper feelings among crowded tourists.

Independent work presented by me | Feb. - May 2016
AR interactions developed in Unity3d using C#, assets made in 3DsMax
Project completed as my Bachelor degree project under the instruction of Chaohui Lv and Xingyun Yang | Thesis publication
