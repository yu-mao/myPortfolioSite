For an international and entrepreneurial alumni community in digital tech industries 

I graduated from a EIT Digital Master's programme (major in Human-Computer Interaction and Design, minor in Innovation and Entrepreneurship). After graduation, I applied and was elected to be the Marketing and Communications Officer (MarComms) of its alumni community - EIT Digital Alumni Foundation, joining the collective efforts to build "the global community of Europe's tech innovators", as we aim to be. 

Apart from the above, during my mandate,
I also designed a brandbook,
published an annual report,
managed social media accounts (LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, FaceBook),
and assisted to produce a marketing video.

Represented at community events in various locations | Berlin, Budapest, Brussels, Madrid, Munich, Stockholm
Work conducted by me in a two-year mandate | July 2019 - June 2021

Communication channel managed with HootSuite, Bitly, Mailchimp, Drupal and Google Analytics
Graphics designed with Adobe Creative suite and Figma
