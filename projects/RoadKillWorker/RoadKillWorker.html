RoadKill Worker

A FPS game prototype hailing from a weekend game jam.  

Project was developed at a weekend game jam organised by LiTHe kod at Linköping, Sweden as a local event of Global Game Jam 2020 with a theme of REPAIR. 


It was January 2020, a coronavirus pandemic started hitting the world hard.

Imagine the situation gets escalated, and you become one of the human-beings left on the earth. To keep the remaining distributed people connected, you are tasked to repair a broken transport system.
