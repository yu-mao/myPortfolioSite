Gaze-typing for Everyday Use

Interaction Prototype, HCI Research, User Test

Project was conducted as my master thesis (grade A, excellent) at KTH Royal Institute of Technology (Sweden) and Aalto University (Finland) with Tobii Dynavox R&D team (Sweden) for people with speech and motor impairments as augmentative and alternative communication

Independent work presented by me | Feb. - Oct. 2018
User study data analysis used python, keyboard prototype using C#/.NET/WPF/MVVM, graphic illustration using Figma and Photoshop
